Sponsorship packages

There are 3 combined sponsorship packages (Gold, Silver or Bronze) that cover the BeNeLux chapter meetings 2023 and the BeNeLux OWASP Days 2022.

The sponsor brochure will soon be available.

Your sponsorship will be invested directly in the chapter meetings, supporting speaker and catering expenses.

The sponsorship will also be dedicated to cover the costs of the OWASP BeNeLux 2023 event.
